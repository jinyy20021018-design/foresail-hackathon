## Design Context

### Users
ForeSail is used by trade operations, risk, and compliance teams who scan active shipments, identify material exposure, and decide what needs action before a contractual deadline. Most work happens on desktop during active case review, with mobile used for quick status checks.

### Brand Personality
Precise, calm, and accountable. The interface should create confidence under time pressure without making every case feel like an alarm.

### Aesthetic Direction
A light, restrained product interface with tinted neutral surfaces and royal blue for navigation and actions. Red, amber, and green are reserved for semantic risk states. The dark navy route map is the signature contrast surface. Avoid decorative gradients, magenta, excessive cards, and dashboard ornament.

### Design Principles
1. Put decisions before fields: status, exposure, deadline, and required action lead each view.
2. Group related facts into scan patterns instead of giving every field equal visual weight.
3. Keep dense operational data readable through alignment, short labels, and stable column rhythm.
4. Use color only for navigation, action, and semantic state; never as decoration.
5. Preserve familiar product affordances and make deeper evidence progressively available.
